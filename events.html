ConsenSys+Balanc3+Token Foundry Beijing Meetup

A ConsenSys formation, Token Foundry is the global platform for launching new token economies and safely issuing tokens. With an emphasis on fraud prevention, consumer protection, and regulatory compliance, Token Foundry aims to bring trust, transparency, and equal access to tokens and token sales. For those who qualify to launch on our platform, Token Foundry provides comprehensive infrastructure & advisory services to help best position these new token economies for long term success.

Blockchain EXPO Europe 2018 in Netherlands

Blockchain EXPO Europe 2018 was successfully held at the RAI Exhibition Center in Amsterdam from June 27th to 28th, 2018. This expo is the largest blockchain event in European history. More than 8,000 blockchain experts have come to share the blockchain cutting-edge technologies and projects, which attracted more than 12,000 visitors to attend this expo. As an open source blockchain community, B2 also participated in this expo.
