MIX Labs

MIX Labs is a research laboratory that focuses on artificial intelligence and blockchain. The headquartered of MIX Labs is in Beijing, and it also has research centers in China, the United States, and Switzerland. The members of MIX Labs team are technical geeks from top universities and research institutions such as Peking University, Tsinghua University, Harvard University, Carnegie Mellon University and Microsoft Research Asia, and also R&D personnel from multinational enterprises such as IBM, Intel, Microsoft, etc.

hihoCoder

hihoCoder is a Chinese online evaluation system for the recruitment of programmers. This system allows applicants to write programs online and solve programming problems. After which the system will evaluate the codes submitted by the applicants and send a detailed test report to the hiring organization.
Founded in December 2012, hihoCoder includes exercise, competition, and discussion, where users can improve their levels through practicing programming questions with different levels of difficulty. A competition is held every month to enable users to test their level and it also offers a forum for them to share different experiences. hihoCoder has also become the platform for the Microsoft 2014 internship recruiting program.

Beihang SHIXIN Studio

SHIXIN Studio is an online media laboratory led by the Shi’e School of Beihang University. It is dedicated to solving campus problems with cutting-edge technology, focusing on making more college students get interests in technology and use technology in a better way. They have completed several campus projects and held a 24h blockchain Hackathon, which provides a platform for technology creators of different universities.

YOUNG Blockchain Youth Community

YOUNG Blockchain Youth Community was built by several college students who aim to provide a platform for young students to learn and exchange blockchain information. The members of YOUNG Blockchain Youth Community come from universities like Tsinghua University, Peking University, Stanford University, MIT, Renmin University of China, Beijing Normal University, and Beijing Institute of Technology, etc.

Tokenview

Tokenview is the first multi-cryptocurrency query blockchain explorer, which aims to provide clients with a one-stop information query service by using the world’s latest blockchain technology. Tokenview supports the second level query of tens of thousands of mainstream electronic money block data. By continuously supporting new crypto-currencies and adding new features, Tokenview will always be the most powerful and professional blockchain explorer in the world.
